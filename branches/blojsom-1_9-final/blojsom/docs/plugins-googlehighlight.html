description

The Google Highlight plugin will highlight words on your blog if the referer came from a Google query.

installation and setup

In your plugin.properties file, add the following line: 

    google-highlight=org.ignition.blojsom.plugin.highlight.GoogleHighlightPlugin
You may then add "google-highlight" to one of the flavor-based plugin chains. For example: 
    html.blojsom-plugin-chain=google-highlight

context attributes and types

No context attributes are made available or used by the Google Highlight plugin.
