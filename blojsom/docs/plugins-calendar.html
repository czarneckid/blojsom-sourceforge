installation and setup

In your plugin.properties file, add the following line: 

    calendar=org.ignition.blojsom.plugin.calendar.CalendarPlugin
You may then add "calendar" to the begining of one of the flavor-based plugin chains. For example: 
    html.blojsom-plugin-chain=calendar,...
